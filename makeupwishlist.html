Makeup Wishlist


View on Github

Overview

As a person who loves makeup and the creative process that comes with it, I decided to create an application in which users can add makeup items to their wishlist. Users can scroll through the different categories and add an item to their wishlist. They can equally remove a products from their wishlish or simply empty their entire wishlist.



When I add an item, I am shown a message indicating which item was added. I can change the title by using the textbox on the top left.



When I click on "View wishlist", I can view all of the products in my wishlist.
