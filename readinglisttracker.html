Overview

Reading is one of my favourite hobbies. However, as a programming student, there is much studying and homework to do so books have accumulated in my library to be read! The idea behind this mobile application is to keep track of books that have yet to be started, continued, or finished reading (so that they may be recommend them to other people).

On the first page, users are able to enter the information of the books they want to add to their reading list.
On the second page, users are able to see the books in their reading list.
On the third page, users are able to see the number of books that are not yet started, in progress, or completed.



When users click on a book, they are able to edit, update or remove books.
